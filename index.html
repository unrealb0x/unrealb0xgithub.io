    About me
    & the project

    Hi! I'm Martin Villarreal, an artist and visual designer from Argentina. This is my personal project where I explore and share crazy, weird, and out-of-the-box ideas, concepts, and content.

    I use Unrealbox as a space for play and experiment; my work draws inspiration from an eclectic mix of sources including nature, technology, architecture, and pop culture.

    The evolution and growth of this project have been largely fueled by the support of the Passengers who collect my pieces. I hope that my content resonates with you and fills your bored screen with what I enjoy creating. Thank you!
